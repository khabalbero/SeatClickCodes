FAQS
1. How does SeatClick work?
SeatClick is an online booking system. This means that you can get confirmation of seats directly from Power Transit Inc. 24 hours after your reservation.
2. How much is the fee to book our bus ticket on SeatClick?
SeatClick only charges a minimal convenience booking fee of P30 per ticket.
3. When should I pay for my transaction?
Please settle your payment within 48 hours. Your reservation will automatically expire if we do not receive confirmation of payment from you within this period. You may rebook your trip if seats are still available.
4. What are my payment options?
We offer the following payment options:.
  • Bank deposits (BPI)
  • GCash
Please note that minimal addition fees may apply depending on the payment option chosen.
5. Can I rebook or cancel my booking?
SeatClick allows cancellations and rebookings of existing bookings through its website. There is no refund for the website convenience fee. However, refund for the bus tickets depends on the policy of the bus operator where the booking was made. The specific terms for this policy should be provided and agreed on before payment.
6. How early should I be at the terminal?
Once you receive your e-ticket after payment, you will just need to show this at the terminal. Please be there 30 minutes before your scheduled trip.
7. Can I avail a PWD, Senior Citizen and Student Discount?

SeatClick allows discounts on Persons with Disabilities(PWDs), Senior Citizen and Students discount. You just need to select the category you are in and just kindly present your ID to serve as a proof together with your e-ticket marked as the same category to avail the discount.

Note that Seatclick gives priorities to PWDs, Senior Citizens, Students and Pregnant Women on seat occupancy and reservations.

8. Do you offer roundtrip booking?
At the moment, we have disabled roundtrip booking for operational purposes. You simply need to book your departure and arrival trips separately and easily.
